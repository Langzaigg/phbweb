DRAGONRIDER

龙骑士

The Dragonrider

Some dream of soaring through the clouds atop a mighty draconic steed, feeling the wind on their faces, afraid of nothing. The dragonrider doesn’t simply dream of this life-he leads it.

有的人会梦到自己骑着巨龙一样的坐骑翱翔在云端，感受风拂过脸颊，没有任何事情能让他恐惧。龙骑士不仅仅是生命中的一个梦--他能自己掌握。

Characters of all classes may become dragonriders, though the most common are barbarians, fighters, paladins, and rangers. Other characters looking to follow this path often pick up a level of one of these classes first, to gain the riding talent necessary.

任何职业都能成为一个龙骑士，尽管通常大部分是野蛮人、战士、圣武士或者巡林客。那些准备踏上龙骑士道路的人物往往会先提升这些职业（前文提到的野蛮人、战士、圣武士或者巡林客），以获得必要的骑乘技能。

An NPC dragonrider might be encountered as a solitary ally of a family of dragons or as part of a larger fighting force, perhaps affiliated with a kingdom or city-state that uses dragons as part of its defenses.

一个NPC龙骑士可能是一个孤独的巨龙家族的盟友，或是一场大战中的一员，也可能是将巨龙作为一个国家或是城市的防御力量的联系者。

Hit Die: d10.

生命骰：D10

Requirements

进阶要求：

To qualify to become a dragonrider, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.

要获得成为龙骑士的资格，人物必须满足以下标准。

Base Attack Bonus: +5.

基本攻击加值：+5

Feats: Mounted Combat, Skill Focus (Ride).

专长：骑乘战斗，技能专攻（骑乘）

Skills: Diplomacy 4 ranks, Handle Animal 4 ranks, Ride 8 ranks.

技能：交涉4级，驯养动物4级，骑乘8级

Language: Draconic.

语言：龙语

Class Skills:

职业技能：

The dragonrider’s class skills (and the key ability for each skill) are Balance (Dex), Concentration (Con), Diplomacy (Cha), Handle Animal (Cha), Jump (Str), Ride (Dex), Spot (Wis), and Tumble (Dex).

龙骑士的本职技能（和各技能的关键属性）是：平衡（敏捷），专注（体质），交涉（魅力），驯养动物（魅力），跳跃（力量），骑乘（敏捷），侦察（感知），翻滚（敏捷）

See Chapter 4: Skills in the Player’s Handbook for skill descriptions.

见玩家手册第四章：技能关于技能的描述。

Skill Points at Each Level: 2 + Int modifier.

每级技能点：2+智力加值。

Class Features

职业特性

Weapon and Armor Proficiency: A dragonrider gains proficiency with the lance, longspear, short bow, and all simple weapons. Dragonriders are proficient with light, medium, and heavy armor and with shields.

武器和防具擅长：龙骑士擅长使用长枪、长矛、短弓，以及所有的简单武器，同时擅长轻甲、中甲、重甲和盾牌。

Dragonriding (Ex): A dragonrider may add his class level as a bonus to any Ride checks made in conjunction with riding a dragon. In addition, any dragon ridden by a dragonrider enjoys maneuverability of one grade better than normal (maximum perfect maneuverability). For instance, an adult green dragon with a dragonrider astride it has average maneuverability rather than poor.

骑乘巨龙（Ex）：龙骑士骑龙进行骑乘检定时，能获得与他的龙骑士职业等级相等的加值。另外，任何被龙骑士骑乘的巨龙可以享受比正常高一级的机动性（机动性的最大值是完美）。例如，龙骑士骑着一头成年绿龙，它拥有比通常笨拙更好的机动性。

Immune to Frightful Presence (Su): While mounted on or within 10 feet of his dragon mount, a dragonrider is immune to the frightful presence of dragons.

免疫龙威（Su）：当骑着或是离坐骑龙在10尺之内时，龙骑士能免疫龙威。

Mounted Spellcasting (Ex): A dragonrider has a +5 bonus on Concentration checks made to cast a spell while riding a mount.

骑乘施法（Ex）：龙骑士在骑龙施法时在专注检定上能获得+5奖励。

Bonus Feat: At 3rd level, a dragonrider gains a bonus feat drawn from the following list: Mounted Archery, Ride-By Attack, Spirited Charge, Trample, Weapon Focus (lance), Weapon Specialization (lance). He must meet all theprerequisites for this bonus feat.

奖励专长：3级时，龙骑士可以从下面的列表中获得一个奖励专长：骑乘射击、快速骑乘攻击，奋力冲刺，践踏，武器专攻（长枪），武器专精（长枪）。他必须具备先决条件才能获得相应的奖励专长。

Flyby Attack (Ex): Any dragon mount ridden by a dragonrider of at least 4th level is treated as having the Flyby Attack feat.

飞越攻击（Ex）：任何被4级以上龙骑士骑着的坐骑龙被视为拥有飞越攻击专长。

Spur Mount (Ex): A 5th-level dragonrider can make a DC 20 Ride check to spur his dragon mount to greater speed. Success on this check increases the dragon’s speed (flying and otherwise) by 50% (round down to the nearest 5-foot increment), for 5 rounds.

鞭策坐骑（Ex）：一个5级龙骑士能通过一个DC20的骑术检定来鞭策他的坐骑龙加快速度。成功通过检定能提高龙的50%（舍去尾数取最近5尺增量）的速度（飞行等）
