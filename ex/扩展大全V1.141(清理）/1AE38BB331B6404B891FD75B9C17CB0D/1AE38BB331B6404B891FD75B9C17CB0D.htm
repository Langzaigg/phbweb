Not all who explore the mysterious powers of dragons do so out of heritage or religious faith.&nbsp; Some become students of knowledge that leads to greater power.

不是所有研究巨龙神秘力量的人都能像这样脱离传统和宗教信仰。一些人成为这种知识的研究者，并且向那伟大的力量一步步靠近。

Most initiates of the draconic mysteries are monks, though some fighters follow the path as well. In rare cases, barbarians, rangers, or rogues have been known to become initiates.

大多数龙之传人是武僧，尽管一些战士同样追随这种信仰。因为某些极少的原因，野蛮人、巡林客或是游荡者也会成为一名龙之传人。

NPC initiates may gather in quiet places of study to share their learning and practice their techniques. Or they may test their powers in the field, either alone or with others who respect their ways. Regardless of her company, the initiate of the draconic mysteries remains a strange and wondrous individual, set apart from others of her race by her pursuit of ancient secrets.

NPC龙之传人尽量聚集在一起学习来分享他们的学识以及实践他们的技巧。他们也会独自或者与其他尊重他们道路的人一起，在战斗中检验他们的力量。不论在任何队伍中，龙之传人都是一个怪异和令人惊奇的个体，她所追寻的远古秘密使得她与她的同胞产生了隔阂。

Hit Die: d8.

生命骰：D8

Requirements

进阶要求：

To qualify to become an initiate of the draconic mysteries, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.

要获得成为龙之传人的资格，人物必须满足以下标准。

Feats: Alertness, Improved Unarmed Strike, Power Attack.

专长：警觉，精通徒手击打，猛力攻击

Skills: Concentration 6 ranks, Jump 8 ranks, Knowledge (arcana) 6 ranks, Knowledge (religion) 4 ranks, Tumble 4 ranks.

技能：专注6级，跳跃8级，知识（神秘）6级，知识（宗教）4级，翻滚4级。

Language: Draconic.

语言：龙语

Class Features

职业特性

All of the following are class features of the initiate of the draconic mysteries prestige class.

下面的是龙之传人这个进阶职业的特性。

Weapon and Armor Proficiency: An initiate of the draconic mysteries gains no proficiency with any weapons, armor, or shields.

武器和防具擅长：龙之传人不擅长任何武器、盔甲或是盾牌。

Evasion (Ex): An initiate of the draconic mysteries can avoid even magical and unusual attacks with great agility. If an initiate makes a successful Reflex saving throw against an attack that normally deals half damage on a save (such as a red dragon’s fiery breath or a&nbsp; fireball), the initiate instead takes no damage. Evasion can only be used if the initiate is wearing light armor or no armor.

反射闪避（Ex）：即使是魔法和特殊攻击，龙之传人也能敏捷地躲过去。有些攻击效果若通过反射检定可以减少一半伤害（比如红龙的火焰吐息或是一个火球术），如果龙之传人遭到此类攻击，在通过反射检定后他将不受伤害。反射闪避只能在龙之传人穿轻甲或是不穿甲的时候使用。

Claws of the Dragon (Su): At 2nd level, the unarmed strike of an initiate of the draconic mysteries is empowered with draconic might. The initiate’s unarmed strike can overcome damage reduction as if it were a magic weapon. In addition, the unarmed strikes of an initiate of the draconic mysteries may deal slashing damage, at her option. Such damage cannot be nonlethal damage.

龙之爪（Su）：2级时，龙之传人的徒手攻击获得龙的威力。龙之传人的徒手攻击能像魔法武器一样克服伤害减免。另外，龙之传人的徒手攻击由她选择能造成挥砍伤害，正常情况下徒手攻击是钝击伤害，当选择作为挥砍伤害时，无法造成瘀伤。

Keen Senses (Ex): At 3rd level, an initiate gains darkvision out to 60 feet and low-light vision.

感官敏锐（Ex）：3级时，龙之传人将拥有60尺黑暗视觉和昏暗视觉。

Increased Unarmed Damage (Ex): At 4th level, the damage dealt by an initiate’s unarmed attacks increases by one die step (such as from 1d3 to 1d4, or from 1d8 to 1d10). At 8th level, it increases another die step.

徒手伤害加强：4级时，龙之传人徒手造成的伤害增加一级伤害骰（比如从1d3变成1d4，或是从1d8变成1d10）。8级时能再提升一级伤害骰。

Frightful Presence (Ex): A 5th-level initiate of the draconic mysteries can unsettle foes with her mere presence. The initiate can activate her frightful presence as a free action. Creatures within a radius of 30 feet per point of Charisma modifier (minimum 30 feet) are subject to the effect if they have fewer Hit Dice than the initiate (dragons are immune to the effect).

A potentially affected creature that succeeds on a Will save (DC 10 + initiate’s class level + initiate’s Cha modifier) remains immune to that initiate’s frightful presence for 24 hours. On a failure, creatures with 4 or fewer HD become panicked for 2d6 rounds and those with 5 or more HD become shaken for 2d6 rounds.

震慑威势（Ex）：5级时，龙之传人仅仅是出现就能使她的敌人感到不安。龙之传人能用一个自由动作激发她的震慑威势。以她为中心半径为每魅力加值30尺的范围内（最小半径30尺）的生物，如果生命骰低于龙之传人将会被影响。

一个可能会被影响的生物如果成功通过意志豁免（DC10+龙之传人等级+龙之传人的魅力加值），24小时内将免疫龙之传人的震慑威势。若是失败，生命骰小于或等于4的生物将惊慌2d6轮，生命骰大于或等于5的生物则颤栗2d6轮。

Improved Evasion (Ex): At 6th level, an initiate’s evasion ability improves. She still takes no damage on a successful Reflex saving throw against attacks such as a dragon’s breath weapon or a&nbsp; fireball, but henceforth she only takes half damage on a failed save.

精通反射闪避（Ex）：6级时，龙之传人的反射闪避能力获得提高。她在对抗类似于火球术或吐息时，成功通过反射豁免的依旧不会受到伤害，但即使失败了也只受到一半伤害

Spell Resistance (Su): Beginning at 7th level, an initiate of the draconic mysteries gains spell resistance equal to 15 + her class level.

法术抗力（Su）：从7级开始，龙之传人获得等于15+她龙之传人等级的法术抗力。

Deadly Strike (Su): A 9th-level initiate deals triple damage on a critical hit inflicted by her unarmed strike, regardless of whether it dealt bludgeoning or slashing damage.

致命打击（Su）：一个9级的龙之传人在徒手攻击形成重击时能造成3倍伤害，不管她是用钝击伤害还是挥砍伤害。

Timeless Body (Ex): After attaining 9th level, an initiate no longer takes ability score penalties for aging (see Table 6-5: Aging Effects, page 109 of the Player’s Handbook) and cannot be magically aged. Any penalties she may have already taken, however, remain in place. Bonuses still accrue, and the initiate still dies of old age when her time is up.

永恒之身（Ex）：在达到9级之后，龙之传人不再受到年龄处罚（见表6-5：年龄影响，玩家手册109页）也不会被魔法造成老化。她之前已有的处罚，将被保留。奖励产生后，龙之传人依然会在时限到来时死去。

Dragon Shape (Su): At 10th level, an initiate of the draconic mysteries gains the ability to use a shapechange ability once per day to take the form of a dragon, from Tiny to Huge size, for 1 hour. Once a form is assumed, it cannot be changed except to return to normal (which dismisses the effect). The effect is otherwise identical to the shapechange spell, including the HD limitation of the new form and the abilities of the form gained.

巨龙之形（Su）：在10级，龙之传人获得每天一次的变形成巨龙1小时的能力，从微型到巨型都可以。一旦变成一个形态，除非还原回正常形态（解除这个效果），否则无法被改变。除上述说明的部分以外，这个效果都与形体变化（Shapechange）法术相同，包括新形态的生命骰上限和异能。
